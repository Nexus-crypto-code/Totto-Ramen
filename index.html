Welcome to Totto Ramen

We bring the warmth of Tokyo ramen culture straight to your bowl with our signature chicken-based broth and hand-picked ingredients.

Our Chicken Ramen Recipe is by acclaimed Ramen Chef, Hideto “Hide” Kawahara. From the broth to the noodles to our own special soy sauce made in Fukuoka, Japan, Totto Ramen brings you an exclusive and unique ramen you can’t find anywhere else.
